If you are visiting from out of town, here are some of my favorite places in the area.

Restaurants

Lakewood Ranch / Sarasota

Lucky Pelican

If you are looking to get your Florida seafood fix, look no further.

Libby's

Modern American cuisine in an upscale-casual setting. I used to work there!

Good Liquid

Beautiful atmosphere, great for cocktails, the flatbread pizza is amazing. Located at Waterside, a new area for shops and restaurants. Make sure you sit outside. I also used to work here!

Ed's Tavern

Bar food, amazing drinks. Sit on the back patio outside. Located at Lakewood Ranch Main Street. Walk around main street after, maybe check out The Fishing Hole mini golf next door.

Poblanos Mexican Grill & Bar

Amazing margaritas, queso, guac. Ask for the flour tortilla chips instead of the regular chips. So good.

Woody's River Roo

Live music(most nights), beautiful view of the river, next to the Ellenton Outlet Mall.

Tampa / St. Pete

Whiskey Joe's

Beautiful view, right on the water, tiki bar on the beach. Sit inside or outside because inside there is a wall of windows so you can see the view no matter where you sit.

Sugar Factory

Crazy cocktails, celebrities go there a lot, great food. Located inside the Hard Rock Casino. Stop at Aubi & Ramsa next door for alcoholic ice cream after dinner. My favorite flavor is Key Lime Martini.

Yard House

The spinach dip appetizer is amazing! Great food, drinks, atmosphere. Very popular right now.

Orlando

Sugar Factory

Reservation required! Crazy cocktails, celebrities go there a lot, great food.

Chocolate Emporium

Located at Universal City Walk (No Universal pass required). Very cool place, crazy milkshakes. Reservation recommended.

Activities

Lakewood Ranch / Sarasota

TreeUmph!

Adventure through the trees. Ziplines and obstacles and rope courses.

St. Armands Circle

Shops, restaurants, the beach.

UTC Mall

Shopping heaven. Every store and restaurant you can imagine. Jeni's Ice Cream is the best ice cream ever! My favorite flavor is Lemon Bar.

Van Wezel

Sarasota isn't called the city of the arts for no reason. Go see a musical or a show. Located at downtown Sarasota.

Lido Key Beach

This is the beach that the locals go to. If you want to be able to park and get out to the beach faster, this is the one to go to.

Anna Maria Island

The island has all kinds of shops and restaurants and a beautiful beach. Slightly touristy but not as bad as Siesta Key. Make sure it is not going to rain because the island floods easily.

Tampa / St. Pete

Clearwater Beach

Beautiful beach, great area, lots of things to do. Check out Frenchy's if you get hungry (order the pretzel appetizer).

The Candle Pour

There are locations in Tampa and St. Pete. You can make your own candle!

Hard Rock Casino

This one is pretty self-explantory. I like the Sugar Factory and Jubao Palace Noodle Bar for dinner and Aubi & Ramsa or Sugar Factory for dessert.

Busch Gardens

Fun for the roller coaster lovers. Lots of cool animals to see. More affordable than Disney and Universal.

TopGolf

I call it bowling but for golf. Food, drinks, games to play (whether you know how to golf or not).

Dave and Busters

Great if you have kids. Arcade, billiards, food, drinks.

Sparkman Wharf

A cute area to hang out in downtown Tampa. Restaurants, activities, food trucks, drinks.

Orlando

Disney World

This is a good park to take kids to or if you are a Disney lover.

Universal

This park is better for teenagers or those that like Marvel, Harry Potter, or Jurassic Park.

Legoland

This is not quite in Orlando, but actually in Winter Park. It is great if you have small children.
